# Videos Folder

Place your situp exercise videos here for analysis.

## Supported Formats
- MP4 (`.mp4`)
- AVI (`.avi`)
- MOV (`.mov`)
- MKV (`.mkv`)

## Video Requirements
- **Full Body Visible**: Ensure the person's entire body is in frame
- **Side View Recommended**: Camera positioned to the side works best
- **Good Lighting**: Clear, well-lit footage improves detection accuracy
- **Stable Camera**: Minimal camera movement for better tracking

## Example Usage
1. Place your video file here (e.g., `situp_video.mp4`)
2. In the notebook, update the path:
   ```python
   video_file_path = r"D:\Sports-X\Videos\situp_video.mp4"
   ```
3. Run the cell to analyze the video

## Tips
- Test with short clips first (10-30 seconds)
- Higher resolution videos work better (720p or 1080p)
- Keep the background clear and uncluttered
